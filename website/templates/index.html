Mission Statement

Counselling Worx is committed to providing compassionate service to people.
We believe in the transforming power of change.
We endeavour to respect the individuality, dignity and diversity of all people who access our services.
Counselling Worx holistically fosters wellbeing within individuals, families and our community.

Talk through your problems or worries with a trained professional
Explore new ways of thinking or behaving
Get help & support for individuals, couples, and families in times of need

Types of Counselling

Our counsellors are equipped to work with issues such as:

  • Depression
  • Relationships
  • Marriage
  • Self Esteem
  • Communication
  • Grief
  • Stress
  • Parenting
  • Work place stress
  • Adjusting to new situations
  • Goal Setting
  • Time Management
  • Work / Life balance
  • Life Coaching
  • Interview Preperation

About our Counsellors

The Counselling Worx team believes in the worth and potential of all human beings. We are committed to assisting people in removing barriers that impede the quality of life.
We value professional diligence and lifelong learning and are committed to staying current and relevant to our clients.
Our Counsellors are able to work with a wide range of issues, and can access a variety of modalities including;

  • Cognitive Behaviour Therapy
  • Solution Focused Therapy
  • Person Centred Therapy
  • Interactional drawing therapy
  • Art Therapy
  • Narrative Therapy

All of our Counsellors hold current appropriate qualifications and are members of either NZAC or NZCCA

Ways Of Funding

Counselling Worx currently provide Counselling for the following contracts and organisations.

  • Midlands – G.P referred counselling
  • Northcare – Mild to moderate mental health issues.
  • OCP – Organisational counselling programme.
  • WINZ – (Disability Allowance)
  • Private – Self funded.
    Private fees range between $80 -$ 100 depending on income and counselling required.

Please note we operate on an appointment only basis.
